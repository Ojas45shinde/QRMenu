How It Works

Up and running in 4 steps

No technical skills needed. If you can fill a form, you can set up your digital menu.

01
🏪
Create your restaurant
Login and set up your restaurant profile — name, logo, description, and brand colour. Takes 2 minutes.
💡 Your brand colour appears on the customer menu header automatically
02
🍽
Add your menu
Add categories like "Starters", "Mains", "Drinks" then add items with name, price, description and photo. Or upload your own HTML menu file directly.
💡 You can upload a custom designed HTML file for a unique look
03
📱
Generate QR codes
Create one QR code per table — "Table 1", "Table 2", "Bar Counter". Download PNG and choose from 3 beautiful display templates.
💡 Each table QR tracks how many times it was scanned
04
✅
Customers scan & order
Place the QR on each table. Customers scan with their phone camera — menu loads instantly. They tell the waiter what they want.
💡 No app needed for customers — just the phone camera
